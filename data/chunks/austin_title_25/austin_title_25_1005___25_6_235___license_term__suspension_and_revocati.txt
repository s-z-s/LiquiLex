ยง 25-6-235 - LICENSE TERM; SUSPENSION AND REVOCATION.
(A) Except as otherwise provided by Subsection (B), a license issued under this division is effective on the date of issuance and remains effective through the
end of the calendar year in which it is issued.
(B) If a bond required by this division lapses or is terminated, suspended, or revoked, the license issued to the contractor is automatically suspended. The
contractor may not resume construction described by Section 25-6-231 (License Required) until the city manager reinstates or renews the license or
issues a new license.
Source: Sections 13-5-62 and 13-5-63(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
