ยง 25-6-292 - DESIGN AND CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS.
(A) The design, construction, alteration, or repair of a sidewalk, driveway approach, pavement, appurtenance on public property, or other facility to provide
access to adjoining property must comply with the Transportation Criteria Manual.
(B) The design, construction, alteration, or repair of a curb or gutter must comply with the Drainage Criteria Manual and the Transportation Criteria Manual.
(C) Access to a lot from an alley must be approved by the city manager.
Source: Section 13-5-66(a) and (c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
