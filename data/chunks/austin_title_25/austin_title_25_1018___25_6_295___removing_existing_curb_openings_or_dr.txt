ยง 25-6-295 - REMOVING EXISTING CURB OPENINGS OR DRIVEWAY APPROACHES.
A person who constructs a new driveway approach shall:
(1) remove an existing curb opening or driveway approach on the same property if the opening or approach is not required;
(2) match a new curb, gutter, or sidewalk, to the existing adjoining curb grade and alignment; and
(3) install a new curb, gutter or sidewalk, if required, at the same time that the new driveway approach is installed.
Source: Section 13-5-83(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
