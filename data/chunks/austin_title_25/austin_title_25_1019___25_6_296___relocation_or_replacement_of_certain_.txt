ยง 25-6-296 - RELOCATION OR REPLACEMENT OF CERTAIN FACILITIES OR TREES.
(A) A permittee shall pay the cost of relocating a public utility's stormwater drainage improvement, or tree required by the permittee's proposed
construction.
(B) If relocating or replacing a tree is required as a condition of a right-of-way construction permit, the permit shall comply with the requirements of the
director.
Source: Section 13-5-68; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
