ยง 25-6-323 - ALLEYWAYS.
The director of the Public Works Department may require an existing, unpaved alley to be paved for all or a portion of its length if access from an alley is
proposed in an application for zoning, re-zoning, or site plan approval.
Source: Section 13-5-66(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
