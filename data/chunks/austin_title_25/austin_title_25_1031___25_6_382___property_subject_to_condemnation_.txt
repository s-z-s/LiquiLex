ยง 25-6-382 - PROPERTY SUBJECT TO CONDEMNATION.
On the request of a condemning authority or property owner before acquisition of a right-of-way occurs, the city manager may modify the access requirements
of this division and the Transportation Criteria Manual for a property that is subject to right-of-way condemnation if the modification does not create a public safety
hazard or have an adverse effect on traffic operation.
Source: Section 13-5-84(d); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 010607-50; Ord. 030306-48A; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
