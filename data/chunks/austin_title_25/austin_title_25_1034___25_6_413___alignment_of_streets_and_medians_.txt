ยง 25-6-413 - ALIGNMENT OF STREETS AND MEDIANS.
(A) Except as provided by Subsection (B), the design and construction of a connecting street that intersects with an existing divided hill country roadway must
align with an existing median break on a hill country roadway.
(B) The city manager may approve the construction of a connecting street that does not align with an existing median break if alignment is not practicable.
Source: Section 13-5-85(h); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 010607-50; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
