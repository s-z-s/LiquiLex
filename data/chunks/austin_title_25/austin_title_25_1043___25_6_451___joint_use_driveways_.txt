ยง 25-6-451 - JOINT-USE DRIVEWAYS.
(A) In this division, joint-use driveway means a driveway located entirely or partially on a tract of land that is available for use by an adjoining tract of land as
ingress or egress to a public street.
(B) Vehicular access to a tract of land through a joint-use driveway is permitted as an alternative to direct access to an abutting public or private street.
(C) A joint-use driveway used as alternative access for a single-family residential use may serve not more than eight dwelling units.
Source: Ord. 030306-48A; Ord. 031211-11.
