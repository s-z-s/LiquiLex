(A) A parking or loading facility, circulation area, or queue line must comply with the design and construction standards in this section and in the
Transportation Criteria Manual.
(B) A parking and loading facility must be maintained free of refuse or debris and must be available for the off-street parking or loading use for which the
facility is required.
Source: Section 13-5-105(a) and (f); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
