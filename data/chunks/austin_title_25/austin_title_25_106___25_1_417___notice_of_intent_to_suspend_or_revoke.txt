(A) An accountable official may give notice to the person affected of the official's intent to suspend or revoke a permit, license, released site plan, approved
subdivision construction plan, certificate of occupancy, or right of way use permit under this division.
(B) The notice may specify a reasonable time for compliance with this title. If a time for compliance is specified, the accountable official may not suspend or
revoke before the time for compliance has expired.
Source: Section 13-1-61; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
