ยง 25-6-654 - NOTICE OF CONVEYANCE.
The road utility district shall give written notice to the City of its intent to convey a completed facility. The notice shall be given not later than the 10th day before
the date of a public hearing before the road utility district board of directors, to determine if the facility is completed as specified in the road utility district's
approved plan.
Source: Section 13-1-392; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
