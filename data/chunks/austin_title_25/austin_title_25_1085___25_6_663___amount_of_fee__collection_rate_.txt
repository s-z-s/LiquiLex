ยง 25-6-663 - AMOUNT OF FEE: COLLECTION RATE.
(A) The amount of the street impact fee to be assessed for each service unit and the amount of the street impact fee to be collected for each service unit
shall be set by separate ordinance. The street impact fee to be collected may be increased by ordinance prior to the next scheduled street impact fee
update without amending the street impact fee study, provided that the impact fee to be collected does not exceed the street impact fee that was
assessed.
(B) A street impact fee shall be collected at the time of the issuance of a building permit.
(C) The city manager may enter into an agreement with a developer for a different time and manner of payment of street impact fees.
Source: Ord. No. 20201210-062, Pt. 2, 12-21-20.
