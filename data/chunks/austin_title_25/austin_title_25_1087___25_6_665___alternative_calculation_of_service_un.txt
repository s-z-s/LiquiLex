ยง 25-6-665 - ALTERNATIVE CALCULATION OF SERVICE UNITS.
If an equivalent land use is not found in the LUVMET for the proposed development, an applicant may submit an alternative service unit computation, based
upon a trip generation study as defined by the Institute of Transportation Engineers. The director may use the alternative service unit computation to calculate the
street impact fee.
Source: Ord. No. 20201210-062, Pt. 2, 12-21-20.
