(A) Except as provided in Subsection (B), stormwater runoff shall be computed on the basis of a fully developed contributing drainage area or watershed as
determined under the Drainage Criteria Manual.
(B) When determining the runoff generated from the 500-year flood for the purpose of determining the 100-year floodplain under Subsection (B) of Section
