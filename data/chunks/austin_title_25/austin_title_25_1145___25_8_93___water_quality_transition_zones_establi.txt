ยง 25-8-93 - WATER QUALITY TRANSITION ZONES ESTABLISHED.
(A) In the water supply rural watersheds, water supply suburban watersheds, and in the Barton Springs zone, excluding Lake Austin, Lake Travis, and Lady
Bird Lake, a water quality transition zone is established adjacent and parallel to the outer boundary of each critical water quality zone.
(B) The width of a water quality transition zone is:
(1) for a minor waterway, 100 feet;
(2) for an intermediate waterway, 200 feet; and
(3) for a major waterway, 300 feet.
Source: Sections 13-2-523(a), 13-2-543(a), 13-2-563(a), and 13-2-583(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20131017-046.
