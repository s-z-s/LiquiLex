ยง 25-8-122 - HYDROGEOLOGIC REPORT.
A hydrogeologic report must:
(1) generally describe the topography, soils, and geology of the site;
(2) identify springs and significant point recharge features on the site;
(3) demonstrate that proposed drainage patterns will protect the quality and quantity of recharge at significant point recharge features; and
(4) identify all recorded and unrecorded water wells, both on the site and within 150 feet of the boundary of the site.
Source: Section 13-7-28(1); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20131017-046.
