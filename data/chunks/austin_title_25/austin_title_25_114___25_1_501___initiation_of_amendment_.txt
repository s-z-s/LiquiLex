ยง 25-1-501 - INITIATION OF AMENDMENT.
(A) Other than the city council, only the Planning Commission may initiate an amendment to the regulations in this title.
(B) An amendment to the zoning map may be initiated in accordance with the procedures in Chapter 25-2 (Zoning).
Source: Sections 13-1-980 and 13-1-990; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
