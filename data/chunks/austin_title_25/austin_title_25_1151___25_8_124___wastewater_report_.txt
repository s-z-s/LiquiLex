A wastewater report must:
(1) provide environmental justification for a sewer line location in a critical water quality zone;
(2) address construction techniques and standards for wastewater lines;
(3) include calculations of drainfield or wastewater irrigation areas;
(4) describe alternative wastewater disposal systems used over the Edwards Aquifer recharge zone; and
(5) address on-site collection and treatment systems, their treatment levels, and effects on receiving watercourses or the Edwards Aquifer.
Source: Section 13-7-28(3); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
