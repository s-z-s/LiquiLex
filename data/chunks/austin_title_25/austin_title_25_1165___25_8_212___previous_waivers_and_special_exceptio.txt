ยง 25-8-212 - PREVIOUS WAIVERS AND SPECIAL EXCEPTIONS.
Water quality controls in accordance with Section 25-8-213 (Water Quality Control Standards) are required for a commercial or multifamily development with
more than 20 percent impervious cover that has been granted a waiver of previous water quality requirements or a special exception under this subchapter.
Source: Section 13-2-503(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
