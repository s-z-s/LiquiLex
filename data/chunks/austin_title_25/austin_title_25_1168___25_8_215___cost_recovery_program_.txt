A person who redevelops property in an urban watershed and is required to construct a water quality control may qualify for cost participation by the City for:
(1) construction of the water quality control; or
(2) optional payment instead of construction of the water quality control under Section 25-8-214 (Optional Payment Instead of Structural Controls in
Urban Watersheds).
