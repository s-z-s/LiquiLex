ยง 25-8-282 - WETLAND PROTECTION.
(A) Wetlands must be protected in all watersheds except for wetlands located within the area bounded by Interstate 35, Riverside Drive, Barton Springs Road,
Lamar Boulevard, and 15th Street that are not associated with the critical water quality zone of Lady Bird Lake.
(B) Protection methods for wetlands require the approval of the director, and may include:
(1) appropriate setbacks that preserve the wetlands or wetland functions;
(2) wetland mitigation, including wetland replacement; or
(3) wetland restoration or enhancement.
Source: Section 13-7-21; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20131017-046; Ord. No. 20221027-045, Pt. 27, 11-7-22.
