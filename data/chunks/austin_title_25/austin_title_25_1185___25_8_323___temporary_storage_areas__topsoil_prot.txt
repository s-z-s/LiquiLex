ยง 25-8-323 - TEMPORARY STORAGE AREAS; TOPSOIL PROTECTION.
(A) The site plan or subdivision construction plan must designate the areas to be cleared for temporary storage of spoils or construction equipment. Areas
cleared for temporary storage must be located and restored in accordance with the Environmental Criteria Manual.
(B) During and after site grading operations, the topsoil must be protected and vegetation left in place to the maximum extent practicable.
(C) For areas on the site that are to remain pervious after development, any soils that are compacted during site grading and construction operations must
be decompacted in compliance with the Environmental Criteria Manual and the Standard Specifications Manual.
Source: Section 13-7-15; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20221027-045, Pt. 28, 11-7-22.
