ยง 25-1-533. - VESTED RIGHTS PETITION REQUIRED.
A petition for vested rights that meets the requirements of Section 25-1-534 (Contents of Vested Rights Petition) must be submitted by a landowner or a
landowner's agent in order to request that an application for a permit be reviewed under ordinances, regulations, or rules other than those in effect on the date the
application is filed.
Source: Ord. 20140612-084, Pt. 2, 6-23-14.
