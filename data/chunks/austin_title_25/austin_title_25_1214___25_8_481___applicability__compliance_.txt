ยง 25-8-481 - APPLICABILITY; COMPLIANCE.
(A) This article applies to development in the Barton Springs Zone.
(B) A person who develops in the Barton Springs Zone must comply with the requirements of:
(1) this article; and
(2) Article 13 (Save Our Springs Initiative).
Source: Section 13-2-580 and 15-2-584; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20131017-046.
