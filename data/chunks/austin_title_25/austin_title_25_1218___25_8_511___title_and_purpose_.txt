ยง 25-8-511 - TITLE AND PURPOSE.
(A) This article, to be known as the Save Our Springs Initiative, (SOS hereafter) sets out special requirements for development of land in watersheds within
the City's planning jurisdiction which contribute to Barton Springs.
(B) This article codifies the Save Our Springs Initiative Petition Ordinance as adopted by popular vote on August 8, 1992 and amended by the council.
Source: Section 13-7-36.1; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060216-045.
