ยง 25-8-521 - EFFICIENT AND COST-EFFECTIVE WATER QUALITY PROTECTION MEASURES.
In carrying out City efforts to reduce or remedy runoff pollution from currently developed areas or to prevent runoff pollution from currently developed or
developing areas, the city council shall assure that funds for remedial, retrofit or runoff pollution prevention measures shall be spent so as to achieve the maximum
water quality benefit, and shall assure that the need for future retrofit is avoided whenever feasible.
Source: Section 13-7-36.11; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060216-045.
