ยง 25-8-621 - PERMIT REQUIRED FOR REMOVAL OF PROTECTED TREES; EXCEPTIONS.
(A) Except as otherwise provided in this section, a person may not remove a protected tree unless the Planning and Development Review Department has
issued a permit for the removal under this division.
(B) A person may, without a permit, remove a damaged protected tree that is an imminent hazard to life or property if the tree is removed within seven days
of being damaged. The Planning and Development Review Department may extend this deadline for widespread and extensive storm damage.
(C) A person may, without a permit, remove a protected tree if the tree is identified for removal on an approved preliminary plan, final plat or site plan.
Source: Section 13-7-46; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20100204-038.
