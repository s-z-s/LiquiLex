ยง 25-8-626 - EFFECTIVE DATE AND EXPIRATION OF APPROVAL.
(A) Approval of an application to remove a protected tree is effective immediately.
(B) An approval to remove a protected tree expires:
(1) one year after its effective date, provided that the mitigation conditions in the permit remain in effect until the conditions are met; or
(2) for a development described in Subsection 25-8-621(C) (Permit Required for Removal of Protected Trees; Exceptions), when the development plan
expires.
Source: Section 13-7-41(e); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20100204-038.
