ยง 25-9-5 - REGULATION OF A WASTEWATER TREATMENT PLANT BY THE HEALTH AUTHORITY.
In accordance with Sections 26.173 and 26.177 of the Texas Water Code, the Health Authority:
(1) may inventory, monitor, and periodically inspect and test the discharge from a wastewater treatment plant; and
(2) shall impose on the owner of a plant an annual fee for inspecting and sampling the discharge.
Source: Section 13-3-2(b) and (c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
