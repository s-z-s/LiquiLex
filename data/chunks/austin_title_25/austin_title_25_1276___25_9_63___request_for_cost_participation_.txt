ยง 25-9-63 - REQUEST FOR COST PARTICIPATION.
During the technical review of the application, a determination by the City will be made on whether oversizing of water or wastewater mains or facilities serving
additional property in the area will be necessary. If oversizing of any water or wastewater mains or facilities is necessary, the applicant will be required to submit a
written request for City cost participation to the Director prior to approval of a service extension request.
Source: Section 13-3-17(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20090820-061; Ord. 20130321-065.
