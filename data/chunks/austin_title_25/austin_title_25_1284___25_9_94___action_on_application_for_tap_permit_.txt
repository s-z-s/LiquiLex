ยง 25-9-94 - ACTION ON APPLICATION FOR TAP PERMIT.
(A) On approval of an application for a tap permit, the director of the Water and Wastewater Utility shall issue a written tap permit to the applicant.
(B) If the director of the Water and Wastewater Utility denies a tap permit, the director of the Water and Wastewater Utility must be notify the applicant of
the denial in writing, stating the grounds for the denial.
Source: Section 13-3-6(F); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
