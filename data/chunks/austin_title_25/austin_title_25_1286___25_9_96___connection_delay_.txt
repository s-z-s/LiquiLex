ยง 25-9-96 - CONNECTION DELAY.
(A) The director of the Water and Wastewater Utility may delay an applicant's connection of an exchange or septic tank cutover if the system does not have
sufficient capacity for the connection.
(B) If a connection is delayed under Subsection (A), the director of the Water and Wastewater Utility shall extend the time for connection until there is
sufficient capacity.
Source: Section 13-3A-10(k); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
