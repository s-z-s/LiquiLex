ยง 25-9-132 - DEFINITIONS.
In this article:
(1) LAND USE PLAN means a map showing proposed and existing land uses in a water district.
(2) MUNICIPAL UTILITY DISTRICT means a water district created and operating under the authority of:
(a) Section 59, Article XVI, Texas Constitution; and
(b) Chapters 49, 50 and 54, Texas Water Code.
(3) PETITION means the written request to the city for consent to the creation of or annexation to a water district required by the Texas Water Code and
any document required by City rules.
(4) WATER DISTRICT means a district or authority, including a municipal utility district and a water control and improvement district, that is created
under:
(a) Section 52, Article III, Texas Constitution, or Section 59, Article XVI, Texas Constitution; and
(b) Title 4, Texas Water Code.
Source: Sections 13-1-321 and 13-4-2; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
