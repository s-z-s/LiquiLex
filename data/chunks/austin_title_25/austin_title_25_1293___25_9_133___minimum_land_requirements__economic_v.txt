ยง 25-9-133 - MINIMUM LAND REQUIREMENTS; ECONOMIC VIABILITY.
(A) A water district may not contain less than 100 acres of territory.
(B) A water district must contain an amount of territory sufficient to assure the economic viability of the water district.
(C) An applicant seeking consent to the creation of a water district or to include territory in an existing water district must submit information to demonstrate
the economic viability of the proposed or existing water district.
Source: Section 13-4-3(a) and (c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
