(A) A water district may be created inside the municipal limits of the City only if:
(1) the written consent of the City is obtained;
(2) creation of the water district complies with this article.
(B) Territory located inside the full purpose municipal boundary of the City may be included in a proposed water district only if:
(1) the territory is 1,000 feet or less from a major thoroughfare; and
(2) the area of the territory does not exceed five percent of the total amount of territory in the proposed water district.
Source: Sections 13-4-3(b) and 13-4-10; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
