ยง 25-9-151 - REVIEW OF A PETITION.
(A) The City shall review a petition filed with the City for the City's consent to:
(1) the creation of a water district; or
(2) the annexation of territory to a water district.
(B) The review by the City of the petition must comply with this division.
Source: Sections 13-1-325 and 13-1-331; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
