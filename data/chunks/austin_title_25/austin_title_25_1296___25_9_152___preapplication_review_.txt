ยง 25-9-152 - PREAPPLICATION REVIEW.
(A) A person who intends to file a petition for the creation of a water district may discuss the proposed water district in a preapplication review with City
employees before the petition is filed.
(B) To request a preapplication review, a person must notify the city manager of the person's intent to file a petition in writing at least 30 days before the
date the person files the petition with the City.
Source: Section 13-1-326; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 010607-50; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
