ยง 25-9-163 - SUBDIVISION APPROVAL.
The Land Use Commission may not approve a preliminary plan or final plat of subdivision in a water district that does not comply with a resolution or ordinance
adopted in connection with the consent by the City to the creation of the water district or an agreement entered into in connection with the consent of the City to
the creation of the water district.
Source: Section 13-4-5(c)(4); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11.
