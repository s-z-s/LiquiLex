ยง 25-9-191 - GENERAL PROVISIONS.
(A) The council may impose a condition or restriction on a water district in connection with the consent of the City to the creation of a water district in
accordance with this division and applicable state law.
(B) A condition or restriction imposed in connection with the consent of the City to the creation of a water district must be included in a resolution,
agreement, or ordinance that pertains to the water district.
Source: Section 13-4-5(a) and (b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
