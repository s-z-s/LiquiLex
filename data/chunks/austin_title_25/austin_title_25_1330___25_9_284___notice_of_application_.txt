ยง 25-9-284 - NOTICE OF APPLICATION.
On receipt of an application filed under Section 25-9-283 (Application For Approval of a District Bond) the city manager shall:
(1) notify the council and the presiding officer of the Water and Wastewater Commission of the application; and
(2) send one copy of the application to the presiding officer of the Water and Wastewater Commission.
Source: Section 13-1-371; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 010607-50; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060504-039.
