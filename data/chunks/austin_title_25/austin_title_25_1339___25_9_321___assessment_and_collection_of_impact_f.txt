ยง 25-9-321 - ASSESSMENT AND COLLECTION OF IMPACT FEES AUTHORIZED.
(A) This article levies a water impact fee and a wastewater impact fee to be assessed and collected in accordance with Chapter 395 of the Local Government
Code.
(B) The director of the Water and Wastewater Utility shall collect the water impact fee and the wastewater impact fee on new development in accordance
with Chapter 395 of the Local Government Code and this article.
Source: Section 13-3A-7(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
