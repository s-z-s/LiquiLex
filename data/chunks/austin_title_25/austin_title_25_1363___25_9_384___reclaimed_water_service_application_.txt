ยง 25-9-384 - RECLAIMED WATER SERVICE APPLICATION.
An applicant for a subdivision plat, building permit, site plan, water service extension, or water connection within the service area may submit an application to
use reclaimed water. The director of the Water and Wastewater Utility shall prescribe an application form for reclaimed water service.
Source: Ord. 001214-70; Ord. 031211-11.
