ยง 25-9-396 - PROHIBITIONS.
A user may not:
(1) use reclaimed water for a purpose not approved in the reclaimed water agreement;
(2) use or apply reclaimed water for a purpose, including approved uses, directly or by windblown spray, to an area other than that approved in the
reclaimed water agreement;
(3) use hose bibs on an onsite reclaimed water system unless the director approves the use of hose bibs; or
(4) allow obstructions to impede access to meter boxes or other facilities.
Source: Ord. 001214-70; Ord. 031211-11.
