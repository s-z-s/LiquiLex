ยง 25-9-397 - RATES AND CHARGES.
(A) The Utility shall charge the rate provided by separate ordinance for the following:
(1) reclaimed water fee;
(2) tap fee;
(3) meter set charges;
(4) engineering or inspection fees;
(5) reconnection fee; and
(6) service reinstatement fee.
(B) A user of reclaimed water service must pay an additional fee set by separate ordinance for discharge of reclaimed water to the sanitary sewer.
Source: Ord. 001214-70; Ord. 031211-11.
