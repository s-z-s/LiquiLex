ยง 25-9-399 - BILLING.
(A) The Utility may bill for reclaimed water monthly.
(B) An active account becomes delinquent when full payment is not received in the Utility customer service office by the due date on the bill.
(C) The Utility may discontinue a delinquent account, regardless of location, until the billing is paid.
Source: Ord. 001214-70; Ord. 031211-11.
