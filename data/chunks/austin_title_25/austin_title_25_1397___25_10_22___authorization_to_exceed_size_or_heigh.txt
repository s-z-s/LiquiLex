(A) The building official may authorize installation of a sign that exceeds the applicable size or height restriction by up to 20 percent of the maximum size or
height prescribed by this chapter after determining that:
(1) the sign owner or user has demonstrated the existence of practical difficulties in complying with this chapter;
(2) a unique circumstance exists that makes compliance with the requirements of this chapter impractical;
(3) the modification is in conformity with the purposes of this chapter; and
(4) the modification does not lessen public safety requirements.
(B) The building official shall record the details of a modification authorized under this section in the City files.
Source: Section 13-2-852(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
