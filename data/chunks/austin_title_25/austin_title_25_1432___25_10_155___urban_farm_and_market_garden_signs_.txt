ยง 25-10-155 - URBAN FARM AND MARKET GARDEN SIGNS.
(A) For an urban farm use, a non-electrified sign is permitted that:
(1) is not more than eight square feet in size;
(2) is not more than four feet above grade.
(B) For a market garden use, a non-electrified sign is permitted that:
(1) is not more than four square feet in size; and
(2) is not more than four feet above grade.
