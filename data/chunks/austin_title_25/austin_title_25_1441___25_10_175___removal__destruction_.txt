ยง 25-10-175 - REMOVAL; DESTRUCTION.
(A) The building official or the Electric Utility Department may remove a street banner that:
(1) is displayed after expiration of the street banner display permit; or
(2) in the opinion of the building official or the Electric Utility Department, creates a public safety hazard;
(3) was installed in the public right-of-way without a permit; or
(4) is illegal.
(B) The building official may destroy a street banner:
(1) after the 10th day following the expiration of the street banner display permit, if the street banner is removed under Subsection (A)(1) or (A)(2) and
not reclaimed by the permittee; or
(2) immediately, if the street banner is removed under Subsection (A)(3) or (A)(4).
Source: Sections 13-2-874(k) and (l); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
