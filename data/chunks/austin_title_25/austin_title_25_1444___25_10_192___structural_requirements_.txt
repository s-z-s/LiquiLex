ยง 25-10-192 - STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS.
(A) Except for a wall sign, a sign must be designed, installed, and maintained so that it will withstand a horizontal pressure of 30 pounds per square foot of
exposed surface.
(B) A lighted sign:
(1) may not produce glare visible to vehicle drivers; and
(2) must be visually separated from traffic signs, signals, and devices.
Source: Section 13-2-887; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
