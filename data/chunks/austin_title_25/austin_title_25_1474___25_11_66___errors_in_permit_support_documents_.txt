ยง 25-11-66 - ERRORS IN PERMIT SUPPORT DOCUMENTS.
If the building official discovers an error in the plans, specifications, or other data submitted in support of a permit application, the building official may:
(1) require an applicant to correct the error; and
(2) stop building operations at the site if the error results in a violation of City regulations.
Source: Section 13-1-742; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
Division 3. - Building Permit Issuance, Appeal, Expiration, and Extension.
