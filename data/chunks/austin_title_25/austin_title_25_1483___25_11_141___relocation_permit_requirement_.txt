ยง 25-11-141 - RELOCATION PERMIT REQUIREMENT.
(A) Except as provided in Subsection (B), a person must obtain a relocation permit to move a building regulated by this title from one site to another or along
public right-of-way.
(B) A relocation permit is not required to move a building that:
(1) is specifically designed and constructed to be portable;
(2) has a loaded height of not more than 14 feet and a loaded width of not more than 14 feet.
Source: Section 13-1-745(a)(2) and (g); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
