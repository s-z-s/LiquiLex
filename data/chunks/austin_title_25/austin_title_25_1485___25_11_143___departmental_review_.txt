ยง 25-11-143 - DEPARTMENTAL REVIEW.
(A) The building official shall submit each application for a relocation permit to affected city departments for review. The departments shall provide the
building official with a recommendation on issuance of the permit.
(B) The applicant may amend the application if it is disapproved.
Source: Section 13-1-745(k); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
