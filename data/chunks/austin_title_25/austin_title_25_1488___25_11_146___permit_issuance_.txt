ยง 25-11-146 - PERMIT ISSUANCE.
The building official shall issue a relocation permit if the building official determines that:
(1) the proposed relocation complies with applicable regulations;
(2) the applicant has paid required fees and deposits; and
(3) the applicant has obtained all required building permits.
Source: Sections 13-1-741 and 13-1-745(i)(1); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
