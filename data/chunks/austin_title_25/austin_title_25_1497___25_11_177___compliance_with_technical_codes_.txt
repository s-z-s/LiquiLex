ยง 25-11-177 - COMPLIANCE WITH TECHNICAL CODES.
(A) A building or structure or building service equipment that is moved into or through the City's zoning jurisdiction must comply with the Building Code
requirements for relocated buildings.
(B) A person may not move a substandard or dangerous building into or through the City's zoning jurisdiction.
Source: Section 13-1-745(f) and (p); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
