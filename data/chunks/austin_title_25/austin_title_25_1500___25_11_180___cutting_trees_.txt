ยง 25-11-180 - CUTTING TREES.
A permittee may not cut or trim a tree or shrub located on or over:
(1) a public right-of-way or public land without written permission from the Parks and Recreation Department; or
(2) private property without written permission of the owner or person in control of the property.
Source: Section 13-1-745(e); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
