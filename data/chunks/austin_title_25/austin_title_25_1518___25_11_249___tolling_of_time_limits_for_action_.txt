ยง 25-11-249 - TOLLING OF TIME LIMITS FOR ACTION.
For purposes of the time limits for action in Sections 25-11-213, 25-11-214, and 25-11-245, a postponement requested or agreed to by the owner or his agent
tolls the running of the time limit from the date of the request until the meeting to which the case has been postponed.
