ยง 25-12-55 - OFFENSE AND PENALTY.
(A) A person who violates this article commits a separate offense for each day the violation continues.
(B) A person who fails to comply with a permit or variance granted pursuant to this article commits a separate offense for each day the violation continues.
(C) A person who violates this article or fails to comply with a permit or a variance granted pursuant to this article commits a misdemeanor punishable as set
forth in Section 25-1-462 (Criminal Enforcement).
(Ord. No. 20210603-059, Pt. 4, 9-1-21)
