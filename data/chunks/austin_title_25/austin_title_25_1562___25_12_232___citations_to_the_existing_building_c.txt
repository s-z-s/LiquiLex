ยง 25-12-232 - CITATIONS TO THE EXISTING BUILDING CODE.
In the City Code, "Existing Building Code" means the 2024 International Existing Building Code adopted in Section 25-12-231(International Existing Building Code),
as amended by Section 25-12-233(Local Amendments to the International Existing Building Code). In this article, "this code" means the Existing Building Code.
