ยง 25-13-4 - BOARD OF ADJUSTMENT.
The Board of Adjustment shall, in accordance with Local Government Code Chapter 241, Subchapter C, hear and decide:
(1) an appeal from an order, requirement, decision, or determination made by the director in the enforcement of this chapter; and
(2) a request for a variance from a requirement of this chapter.
Source: Ord. 010809-78; Ord. 031211-11.
