ยง 25-13-42 - RESTRICTION ON LAND USES.
In the controlled compatible land use area, a person may not engage in a land use unless:
(1) the land use is permitted under this article; and
(2) in the zoning jurisdiction, the land use is permitted under Chapter 25-2 (Zoning).
Source: Ord. 010809-78; Ord. 031211-11.
