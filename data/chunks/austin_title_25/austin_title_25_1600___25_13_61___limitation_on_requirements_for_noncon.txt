ยง 25-13-61 - LIMITATION ON REQUIREMENTS FOR NONCONFORMING USES, STRUCTURES, AND OBJECTS.
Except as otherwise provided in this article, this chapter does not require:
(1) a change in a nonconforming land use;
(2) the removal, lowering, or other change of a nonconforming structure or object of natural growth; or
(3) any other interference in the continuation of a nonconforming land use.
Source: Ord. 010809-78; Ord. 031211-11.
