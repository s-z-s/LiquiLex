ยง 25-13-64 - NONCONFORMING STRUCTURES.
(A) Except as otherwise provided in Subsection (B) and Section 25-13-82 (Restriction On Permits), a person may repair, replace, rebuild, or expand a
nonconforming structure. The portion of a nonconforming structure that is replaced, rebuilt, or expanded must comply with the noise level reduction
measures prescribed by Article 3 (Compatible Land Uses), if any.
(B) Maintenance or improvement of a nonconforming structure that is prohibited by Article 3 (Compatible Land Uses) is limited to:
(1) that required by law to comply with minimum health and safety standards; and
(2) the implementation of outdoor-to-indoor noise level reduction measures.
Source: Ord. 010809-78; Ord. 031211-11.
