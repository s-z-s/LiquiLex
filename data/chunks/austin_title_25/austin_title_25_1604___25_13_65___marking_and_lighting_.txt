ยง 25-13-65 - MARKING AND LIGHTING.
The director or the Board of Adjustment may require as a condition of approval of a permit or variance that the owner of a structure or object of natural growth
install, operate, and maintain on the structure or object of natural growth any markers and lights necessary to indicate to aircraft the presence of an airport hazard.
Source: Ord. 010809-78; Ord. 031211-11.
