ยง 25-1-721 - DEFINITIONS.
In this division,
(1) GOVERNMENT-OPERATED AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROGRAM means a program operated by a federal, state, or local department that provides
financial or other form of subsidy for the purpose of providing affordable housing.
(2) HOUSING FOR OLDER PERSONS means housing for households with at least one individual who is at least 62 years of age at the time of initial
occupancy.
(3) MFI means median family income for the Austin metropolitan statistical area.
(4) QUALIFYING DEVELOPMENT means a development certified under Section 25-1-724 (Certification) and participating in the Affordability Unlocked
Bonus Program.
(5) SLEEPING UNIT means a bedroom in a structure that serves as a dwelling unit for seven or more unrelated individuals who share amenities, such as a
kitchen, bathrooms, or living areas.
(6) SUPPORTIVE HOUSING means housing that includes non-time-limited affordable housing assistance with wrap-around supportive services for
individuals experiencing homelessness, as well as other individuals with disabilities.
