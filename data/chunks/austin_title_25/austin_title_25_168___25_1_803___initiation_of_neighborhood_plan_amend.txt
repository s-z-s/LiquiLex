ยง 25-1-803 - INITIATION OF NEIGHBORHOOD PLAN AMENDMENT.
A neighborhood plan amendment may be initiated by:
(1) for an amendment regarding an individual property:
(a) the owner of the subject property;
(b) the council;
(c) the Planning Commission;
(d) the director;
(e) the neighborhood plan contact team for the planning area in which the property is located; or
(2) for an amendment regarding an area-wide or subdistrict-wide recommendation:
(a) the council;
(b) the Planning Commission;
(c) the director; or
(d) the neighborhood plan contact team for the affected neighborhood plan area.
Source: Ord. 20080306-073; 20091217-053.
