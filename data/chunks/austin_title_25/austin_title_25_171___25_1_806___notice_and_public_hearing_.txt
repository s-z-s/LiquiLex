ยง 25-1-806 - NOTICE AND PUBLIC HEARING.
(A) The director shall give notice of the filing of an application for a proposed neighborhood plan amendment under Section 25-1-133 (Notice of Applications
and Administrative Decisions).
(B) The director shall conduct a community meeting on a proposed neighborhood plan amendment prior to the date on which the Planning Commission is
scheduled to consider the amendment. The director shall give notice of the meeting under Section 25-1-132(A) (Notice of Public Hearing).
(C) The Planning Commission and the council shall each hold a public hearing on a proposed neighborhood plan amendment.
(D) This subsection prescribes notice for a public hearing on a proposed neighborhood plan amendment regarding an individual property.
(1) For a hearing before the Planning Commission, the director shall give notice under Section 25-1-132(A) (Notice of Public Hearing).

(2) For a hearing before council, the director shall give notice under Section 25-1-132(B)(2) (Notice of Public Hearing).
(3) The applicant is responsible for the cost of notice, unless the applicant is a neighborhood plan contact team. In that event, the City is responsible for
the cost of notice.
(E) This subsection prescribes notice for a public hearing on a proposed neighborhood plan amendment regarding an area-wide or subdistrict-wide
recommendation.
(1) The director shall give notice of a public hearing before the Planning Commission or council to:
(a) each notice owner of property located within the proposed amendment boundaries;
(b) each City of Austin utility account address within the proposed amendment boundaries; and
(c) each neighborhood plan contact team and registered neighborhood organization within the proposed amendment boundaries and within 500
feet of the proposed amendment boundaries.
(2) The City is responsible for the cost of notice.
