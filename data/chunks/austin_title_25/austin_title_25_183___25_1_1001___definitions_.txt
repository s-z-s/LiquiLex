ยง 25-1-1001 - DEFINITIONS.
In this article:
(A) DIRECTOR means the director of the Planning Department.
(B) AREA PLAN means a long-range policy plan adopted by ordinance as an element of the comprehensive plan that establishes land use policy for a
specific geographic area. Area plan may include a neighborhood plan, district plan, station area plan, small area plan, land use-focused corridor plan,
framework plan, and plans incorporating future land use maps.
Source: Ord. No. 20250522-065, Pt. 1, 6-2-25.
