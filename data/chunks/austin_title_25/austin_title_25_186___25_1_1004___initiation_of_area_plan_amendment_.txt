ยง 25-1-1004 - INITIATION OF AREA PLAN AMENDMENT.
An area plan amendment may be initiated by:
(1) for an individual property amendment:
(a) the owner of the subject property;
(b) the council;
(c) the Land Use Commission; or
(d) the director.
(2) for an area-wide or subdistrict-wide amendment:
(a) the council;
(b) the Land Use Commission; or
(c) the director.
Source: Ord. No. 20250522-065, Pt. 1, 6-2-25.
