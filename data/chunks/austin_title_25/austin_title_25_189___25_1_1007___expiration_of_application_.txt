ยง 25-1-1007 - EXPIRATION OF APPLICATION.
(A) An area plan amendment application expires if the director does not schedule the application for a public hearing:
(1) by the Land Use Commission before the 181st day after the date of filing; or
(2) by the Land Use Commission or council before the 181st day after the date on which the Land Use Commission or council grants an indefinite
postponement of a scheduled public hearing.
(B) Except as provided in Subsection (D), an area plan amendment application expires if the council does not adopt an ordinance before the 361st day after
council closes the public hearing on the application.
(C) Except as provided in Subsection (D), an area plan amendment application initially submitted before the effective date of this section expires 180 days
after the effective date of this section.
(D) An applicant may file one request with the director and one request with council to extend an application that will expire under Subsection (B) or
Subsection (C). The request must be in writing, be filed before the application expires, state good cause for the extension, and be for not more than 180
days.
Source: Ord. No. 20250522-065, Pt. 1, 6-2-25.
