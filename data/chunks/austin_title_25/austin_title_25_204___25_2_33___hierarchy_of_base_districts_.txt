ยง 25-2-33 - HIERARCHY OF BASE DISTRICTS.
(A) The residential, commercial, and industrial base districts in Subsections (B), (C), and (D) of Section 25-2-32 (Zoning Districts And Map Codes) are arranged
in a hierarchy that begins with the LA base district as the most restrictive district and ends with the LI base district as the least restrictive district.
(B) A combining district is classified in the hierarchy of zoning districts according to the base district with which the combining district is combined.
(C) Special purpose base districts are not included in the hierarchy described in this section.
Source: Section 13-2-25; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
