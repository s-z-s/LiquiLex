ยง 25-2-52 - RESIDENTIAL DISTRICT DESIGNATIONS GENERALLY.
There are 16 residential district designations. The designation established for a proposed use is based on:
(1) density;
(2) lot size;
(3) terrain;
(4) environmental conditions;
(5) location;
(6) surrounding uses;
(7) public infrastructure; and
(8) other factors determined to be relevant by the council.
Source: Sections 13-2-40 through 13-2-56; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
