ยง 25-2-53 - LAKE AUSTIN RESIDENCE (LA) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Lake Austin residence (LA) district is the designation for a low density single-family residential use on a lot that is a minimum of one acre and that is located 1,000
feet or less, measured horizontally, from the 492.8 foot topographic contour line on either side of Lake Austin.
Source: Section 13-2-41; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
