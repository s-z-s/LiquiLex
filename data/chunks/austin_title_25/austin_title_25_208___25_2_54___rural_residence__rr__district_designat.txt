ยง 25-2-54 - RURAL RESIDENCE (RR) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Rural residence (RR) district is the designation for a low density residential use on a lot that is a minimum of one acre. An RR district designation may be applied
to a use in an area for which rural characteristics are desired or an area whose terrain or public service capacity require low density.
Source: Section 13-2-42; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
