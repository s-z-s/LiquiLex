ยง 25-2-55 - SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENCE LARGE LOT (SF-1) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Single-family residence large lot (SF-1) district is the designation for a low density single-family residential use on a lot that is a minimum of 10,000 square feet. An
SF-1 district designation may be applied to a use on land with sloping terrain or environmental limitations that preclude standard lot size or to a use in an existing
residential development on a lot that is 10,000 square feet or more.
Source: Section 13-2-43; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
