ยง 25-1-64 - ACTION ON AN APPLICATION; DEADLINE.
(A) The director shall grant or deny an application for a permit or approval required by this title within the timeframe established by state law.
(B) Nothing in this section limits any exceptions to the deadlines provided for in state law.
