ยง 25-2-56 - SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENCE STANDARD LOT (SF-2) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Single-family residence standard lot (SF-2) district is the designation for a moderate density single-family residential use on a lot that is a minimum of 5,750
square feet. An SF-2 district designation may be applied to a use in an existing single-family neighborhood that has moderate sized lots or to new development of
single-family housing on lots that are 5,750 square feet or more.
Source: Section 13-2-44; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
