ยง 25-2-58 - SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENCE SMALL LOT (SF-4A) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Single-family residence small lot (SF-4A) district is the designation for a moderate density single-family residential use on a lot that is a minimum of 3,600 square
feet. An SF-4A district use is subject to development standards that maintain single family neighborhood characteristics.
Source: Section 13-2-46; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
