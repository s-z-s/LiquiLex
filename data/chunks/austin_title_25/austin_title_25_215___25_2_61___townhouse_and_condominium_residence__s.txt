ยง 25-2-61 - TOWNHOUSE AND CONDOMINIUM RESIDENCE (SF-6) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Townhouse and condominium residence (SF-6) district is the designation for a moderate density single family, duplex, two-family, townhouse, and condominium
use that is not subject to the spacing and location requirements for townhouse and condominium use in an SF-5 district. An SF-6 district designation may be applied
to a use in an area with large lots that have access to streets other than minor residential streets. An SF-6 district may be used as a transition between a single
family and multifamily residential use.
Source: Section 13-2-49; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
