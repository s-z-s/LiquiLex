ยง 25-2-63 - MULTIFAMILY RESIDENCE LOW DENSITY (MF-2) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Multifamily residence low density (MF-2) district is the designation for a multifamily use with a maximum density of up to 23 units per acre, depending on unit
size. An MF-2 district designation may be applied to a use in a multifamily residential area located near single family neighborhoods or in an area for which low
density multifamily use is desired.
Source: Section 13-2-51; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
