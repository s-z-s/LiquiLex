ยง 25-2-64 - MULTIFAMILY RESIDENCE MEDIUM DENSITY (MF-3) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Multifamily residence medium density (MF-3) district is the designation for multifamily use with a maximum density of up to 36 units per acre, depending on unit
size. An MF-3 district designation may be applied to a use in a multifamily residential area located near supporting transportation and commercial facilities in a
centrally located area or in an area for which medium density multifamily use is desired.
