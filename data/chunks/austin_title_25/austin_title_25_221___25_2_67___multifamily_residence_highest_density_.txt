ยง 25-2-67 - MULTIFAMILY RESIDENCE HIGHEST DENSITY (MF-6) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Multifamily residence highest density (MF-6) district is the designation for multifamily and group residential use. An MF-6 district designation may be applied to a
use in a centrally located area near supporting transportation and commercial facilities, an area adjacent to the central business district or a major institutional or
employment center, or an area for which the high density multifamily use is desired.
Source: Section 13-2-55; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
