ยง 25-2-68 - MOBILE HOME RESIDENCE (MH) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Mobile home residence (MH) district is the designation for a mobile home residence park and mobile home subdivision use. An MH use is subject to standards
that promote a residential environment and compatibility with adjoining family residence neighborhoods.
