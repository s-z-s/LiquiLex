ยง 25-2-92 - COMMERCIAL DISTRICT DESIGNATIONS GENERALLY.
There are 13 commercial district designations. The designation established for a proposed commercial use is based on:
(1) type of use;
(2) customer base;
(3) location;
(4) surrounding uses;
(5) traffic generation; and
(6) other factors determined to be relevant by the council.
