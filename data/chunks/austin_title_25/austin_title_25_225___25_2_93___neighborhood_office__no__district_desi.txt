ยง 25-2-93 - NEIGHBORHOOD OFFICE (NO) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Neighborhood office (NO) district is the designation for a small office use that serves neighborhood or community needs, is located in or adjacent to a residential
neighborhood and on a collector street that has a width of 40 feet or more, and does not unreasonably affect traffic. An office in an NO district may contain not
more than one use. Site development regulations applicable to an NO district use are designed to preserve compatibility with existing neighborhoods through
renovation and modernization of existing structures.
Source: Section 13-2-61; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
