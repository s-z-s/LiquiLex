ยง 25-2-96 - COMMERCIAL RECREATION (CR) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Commercial recreation (CR) district is the designation for a commercial or recreation use that serves visitors to major recreational areas, including Lake Travis
and Lake Austin. Site development regulations applicable to a CR district use are designed to minimize visual and environmental disruptions of scenic views.
Source: Section 13-2-64; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
