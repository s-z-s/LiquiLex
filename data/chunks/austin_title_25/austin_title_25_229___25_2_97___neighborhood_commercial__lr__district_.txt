ยง 25-2-97 - NEIGHBORHOOD COMMERCIAL (LR) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Neighborhood commercial (LR) district is the designation for a commercial use that provides business service and office facilities for the residents of a
neighborhood. Site development regulations and performance standards applicable to a LR district use are designed to ensure that the use is compatible and
complementary in scale and appearance with the residential environment.
Source: Section 13-2-65; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
