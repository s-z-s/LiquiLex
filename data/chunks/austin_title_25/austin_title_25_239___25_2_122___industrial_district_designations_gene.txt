ยง 25-2-122 - INDUSTRIAL DISTRICT DESIGNATIONS GENERALLY.
There are four industrial district designations. The designation established for a proposed use is based on:
(1) proposed use;
(2) location;
(3) site size; and
(4) other factors determined to be relevant by the council.
Source: Sections 13-2-80 through 13-2-84; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
