ยง 25-1-81 - AUTHORITY TO FILE AN APPLICATION.
A record owner or the record owner's agent may file an application for a permit or approval required by this title. The responsible director or building official may
require an applicant to provide evidence of the applicant's authority to file an application.
Source: Section 13-1-30; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
