ยง 25-2-123 - INDUSTRIAL PARK (IP) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Industrial park (IP) district is the designation for a limited commercial service use, research and development use, administrative use, or manufacturing use that
meets strict development and performance standards and is generally located on a large site or in a planned industrial center.
Source: Section 13-2-81; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
