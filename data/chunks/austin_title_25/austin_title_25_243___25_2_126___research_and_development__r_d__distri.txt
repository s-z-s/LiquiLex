ยง 25-2-126 - RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT (R&D) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Research and development (R&D) district is the designation for a research use located on a site with a campus-style design. An R&D district designation may be
applied to testing services, research warehousing services, or research assembly services. An R&D district use may not include fabrication, processing,
manufacturing, refining, or resource extraction.
Source: Section 13-2-84; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
