ยง 25-2-141 - AGRICULTURAL (AG) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Agricultural (AG) district is the designation for an agriculture use or an agriculture-related use. The purpose of the AG district designation is to preserve areas of
prime agricultural soils, concentrate urban development in and around growth centers, promote compact urban development, and preserve the environment and
open spaces.
Source: Section 13-2-92; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
