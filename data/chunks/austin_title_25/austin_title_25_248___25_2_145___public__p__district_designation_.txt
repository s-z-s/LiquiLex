ยง 25-2-145 - PUBLIC (P) DISTRICT DESIGNATION.
Public (P) district is the designation for a governmental, civic, public service, or public institution use. A P district designation may be applied to a use located on
property used or reserved for a civic or public institutional purpose or for a major public facility, regardless of ownership of the land on which the use is located. A P
district designation may not be applied to government-owned property that is leased to a nongovernmental agency for a use other than a governmental service or
for a use that supports a primary civic or public institutional use.
Source: Section 13-2-94; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
