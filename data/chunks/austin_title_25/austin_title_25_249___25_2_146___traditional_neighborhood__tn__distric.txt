ยง 25-2-146 - TRADITIONAL NEIGHBORHOOD (TN) DISTRICT.
Traditional neighborhood (TN) district is the designation for a compact, mixed-use development that reflects the urban design practices that existed in the United
States from colonial times until the 1940's. The TN district is governed by Chapter 25-3 (Traditional Neighborhood District).
Source: Section 13-2-95; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
