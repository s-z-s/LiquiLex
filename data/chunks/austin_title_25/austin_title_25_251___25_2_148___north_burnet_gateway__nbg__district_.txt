ยง 25-2-148 - NORTH BURNET/GATEWAY (NBG) DISTRICT.
(A) North Burnet/Gateway (NBG) district is the designation for an identified area of existing low density, auto-oriented commercial, warehouse, and industrial
uses that is the subject of an approved master plan for redevelopment of the area into a higher density urban mixed-use neighborhood that is more
pedestrian friendly and takes advantage of the links to commuter rail transit and the area's key position in the urban core.
(B) An NBG designation may be applied only within the North Burnet/Gateway neighborhood plan area.
Source: Ord. 20090312-035.
