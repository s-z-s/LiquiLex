ยง 25-2-163 - CENTRAL URBAN REDEVELOPMENT (CURE) COMBINING DISTRICT PURPOSE.
(A) The purpose of a central urban redevelopment (CURE) combining district is to promote the stability of neighborhoods in the central urban area.
(B) A CURE combining district may be used:
(1) for sustainable redevelopment of homes, multifamily housing, and small businesses;
(2) to accommodate high priority projects that enhance the stability of urban neighborhoods including the development of affordable housing and small
businesses along principal transportation routes that serve a neighborhood;
(3) to improve the natural environment; and
(4) to encourage high quality development with architectural design and proportion compatible with the neighborhood.
Source: Section 13-2-180; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
