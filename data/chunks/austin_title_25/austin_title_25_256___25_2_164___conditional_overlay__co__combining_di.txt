ยง 25-2-164 - CONDITIONAL OVERLAY (CO) COMBINING DISTRICT PURPOSE.
(A) The purpose of a conditional overlay (CO) combining district is to modify use and site development regulations to address the specific circumstances
presented by a site.
(B) A CO combining district may be used to:
(1) promote compatibility between competing or potentially incompatible uses;
(2) ease the transition from one base district to another;
(3) address land uses or sites with special requirements; and
(4) guide development in unique circumstances.
Source: Section 13-2-120; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
