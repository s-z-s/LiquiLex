ยง 25-2-167 - DOWNTOWN CREEKS (DC) OVERLAY DISTRICT PURPOSE AND BOUNDARIES.
(A) The purpose of the downtown creeks (DC) overlay district is to promote public accessibility to the creeks, to promote pedestrian use of the creeks, and to
protect and enhance the scenic character of the creek corridors.
(B) The DC overlay district applies to property within 60 feet of the centerline of creeks that is located within the CBD or DMU base districts.
Source: Section 13-2-175; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
