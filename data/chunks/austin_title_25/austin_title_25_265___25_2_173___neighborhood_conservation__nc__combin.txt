The purpose of a neighborhood conservation (NC) combining district is to preserve neighborhoods with distinctive architectural styles that were substantially
built out at least 30 years before the date an application for an NC combining district classification is filed.
Source: Section 13-2-130; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
