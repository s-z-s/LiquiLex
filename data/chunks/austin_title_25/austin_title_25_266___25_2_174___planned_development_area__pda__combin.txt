ยง 25-2-174 - PLANNED DEVELOPMENT AREA (PDA) COMBINING DISTRICT PURPOSE.
The purpose of a planned development area (PDA) combining district is to:
(1) provide for industrial and commercial uses in certain commercial and industrial base districts; or
(2) incorporate the terms of a planned development area agreement into a zoning ordinance following annexation of a property that is subject to a
planned development area agreement.
Source: Section 13-2-150; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
