ยง 25-2-241 - DISTINCTION BETWEEN ZONING AND REZONING.
(A) Zoning is the initial classification of property as a particular zoning base district. Zoning amends the zoning map to include property that was not
previously in the zoning jurisdiction or that was not previously included in the boundaries of a base district.
(B) Rezoning amends the zoning map to change the base district classification of property that was previously zoned.
Source: Section 13-1-401; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
