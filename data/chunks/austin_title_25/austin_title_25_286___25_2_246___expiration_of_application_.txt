(A) A zoning or rezoning application expires if the director of the Neighborhood Planning and Zoning Department does not schedule the application for a
public hearing:
(1) by the Land Use Commission before the 181st day after the date of filing; or
(2) by the Land Use Commission or council before the 181st day after the date on which the Land Use Commission or council grants an indefinite
postponement of a scheduled public hearing.
(B) Except as provided in Subsection (C), a zoning or rezoning application expires if the council does not adopt an ordinance before the 361st day after
council closes the public hearing on the application.
(C) An applicant may request that the director of the Neighborhood Planning and Zoning Department or council extend an application that will expire under
Subsection (B).
(1) An applicant's request for the director of the Neighborhood Planning and Zoning Department's approval must be in writing and filed before the
application expires.
(2) An applicant may file one request for an extension from council. The request must state good cause for the extension and may be for not more than
180 days.
Source: Section 13-1-406(f) and Section 13-1-409; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11.
