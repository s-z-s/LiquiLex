ยง 25-1-86 - PROCESSING CYCLES.
(A) The responsible director may establish regular cycles for consideration of applications by City staff, boards, commissions, and the council. The city
manager shall advise the council of the creation or change of a cycle.
(B) An established cycle supersedes conflicting requirements of this title, except the requirements relating to the duration of a project and those mandated
by state law.
