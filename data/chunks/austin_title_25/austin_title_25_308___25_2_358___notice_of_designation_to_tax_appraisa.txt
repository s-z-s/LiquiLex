ยง 25-2-358 - NOTICE OF DESIGNATION TO TAX APPRAISAL DISTRICT.
(A) The historic preservation officer shall file with the county tax appraisal district a:
(1) copy of an ordinance zoning property as a historic landmark or historic area combining district; and
(2) notice stating that the council has granted the historic designation.
(B) The historic preservation officer shall mail a copy of the notice described in Subsection (A)(2) to the notice owner by certified mail.
Source: Ord. 041202-16; Ord. 20060112-053.
