ยง 25-1-88 - BOARD AND COMMISSION SCHEDULE.
The city manager shall inform a board or commission of the dates that other boards or commissions are scheduled to consider an application. A board or
commission shall act diligently to finish its review in accordance with the schedule.
Source: Section 13-1-111; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20190822-117, Pt. 7, 9-1-19; Ord. No. 20230831-141, Pt. 10, 9-11-23.
