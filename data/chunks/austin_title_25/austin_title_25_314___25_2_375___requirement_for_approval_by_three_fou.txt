ยง 25-2-375 - REQUIREMENT FOR APPROVAL BY THREE-FOURTHS OF COUNCIL.
If an owner of property in a proposed NC combining district files a written statement protesting the inclusion of the owner's property in an NC combining district,
a separate affirmative vote of three-fourths of all members of the council is required to include the protesting owner's property in an NC combining district.
Source: Sections 13-2-136 and 13-2-137; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
