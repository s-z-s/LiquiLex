The director of the Neighborhood Planning and Zoning Department shall request that the council initiate the rezoning of property in a PUD zoning district if:
A. a preliminary plan or site plan for a portion of the property is not approved within three years after the effective date of the ordinance approving the PUD
zoning classification for the property; or
B. an approved preliminary plan or site plan expires.
