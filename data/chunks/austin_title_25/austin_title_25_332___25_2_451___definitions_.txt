ยง 25-2-451 - DEFINITIONS.
In this division,
(1) COCKTAIL LOUNGE means a cocktail lounge use described in Chapter 25-2 (Zoning) and established on or before October 31, 2024.
(2) CREATIVE SPACE means a use described in Chapter 25-2 (Zoning) that allows one or more of the following occupancies:
(a) art gallery;
(b) art workshop;
(c) cocktail lounge;
(d) cultural services:
(e) performance venue;
(f) personal improvement services; or
(g) theater.
(3) CREATIVE SPACES DISTRICT means an area designated and zoned as density bonus creative spaces (DBCS) combining district.
Source: Ord. No. 20241010-034, Pt. 3, 10-21-24.
