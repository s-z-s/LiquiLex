ยง 25-2-532 - IMPERVIOUS COVER LIMIT EXCEPTIONS.
(A) This section applies to property that had existing development or that was included in a released site plan on March 10, 1996.
(B) Development may exceed the impervious cover limits of this article if the director determines that the amount of impervious cover proposed is the
minimum necessary to comply with the accessibility standards of the Americans with Disabilities Act or Chapter 25-12, Article 1 (Uniform Building Code).
Source: Section 13-2-630; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
