ยง 25-2-534 - QUALIFYING DEVELOPMENT EXCEPTIONS.
(A) In this section, a qualifying development is a development certified under Section 25-1-724 (Certification) and participating in the Affordability Unlocked
Bonus Program.
(B) A qualifying development is not subject to Section 25-2-511 (Dwelling Unit Occupancy Limit).
(C) Minimum lot size for a qualifying development is 2,500 square feet.
(D) Minimum lot width for a qualifying development is 25 feet.
(E) A Type I development may:
(1) construct to a height that is the applicable base zoning district height limit multiplied by 1.25;
(2) reduce front yard setbacks by 50 percent;
(3) reduce rear setbacks by 50 percent; and
(4) include six dwelling units if the existing zoning on the site is Single Family Residential Small (SF-4A), Single Family Residence Condominium Site (SF-4B),
or more restrictive.
(F) In addition to Subsection (E), a Type 2 development may:
(1) construct to a height that is the applicable base zoning district height limit multiplied by 1.5; and
(2) include eight dwelling units if the existing zoning on the site is Single Family Residential Small (SF-4A), Single Family Residence Condominium Site (SF-
4B), or more restrictive.
