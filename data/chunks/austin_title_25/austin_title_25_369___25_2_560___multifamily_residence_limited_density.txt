ยง 25-2-560 - MULTIFAMILY RESIDENCE LIMITED DENSITY (MF-1) DISTRICT REGULATIONS.
(A) This section applies in a multifamily residence limited density (MF-1) district.
(B) The minimum site area for each dwelling unit is:
(1) 2,500 square feet, for an efficiency dwelling unit;
(2) 3,000 square feet, for a one bedroom dwelling unit; and
(3) 3,500 square feet, for a dwelling unit with two or more bedrooms.
