ยง 25-2-562 - MULTIFAMILY RESIDENCE MEDIUM DENSITY (MF-3) DISTRICT REGULATIONS.
(A) This section applies in an MF-3 district.
(B) The minimum site area for each dwelling unit is:
(1) 1,200 square feet, for an efficiency dwelling unit;
(2) 1,500 square feet, for a one bedroom dwelling unit; and
(3) 1,800 square feet, for a dwelling unit with two or more bedrooms.
Source: Section 13-2-641; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20111215-096.
