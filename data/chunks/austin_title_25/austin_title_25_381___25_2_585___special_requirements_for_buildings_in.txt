ยง 25-2-585 - SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR BUILDINGS IN CERTAIN COMMERCIAL DISTRICTS.
(A) This section applies to a building in a general office (GO), neighborhood commercial (LR), general commercial services (CS), or commercial services - liquor
sales (CS-1) district that:
(1) is located on property adjacent to an Lake Austin residence (LA), rural residence (RR), or single-family residence large lot (SF-1) district; and
(2) exceeds a height of 35 feet.
(B) A building's exterior glass is required to be either clear or lightly tinted.
(C) Exterior light illuminating a building above the second floor is prohibited.
Source: Section 13-2-607; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
