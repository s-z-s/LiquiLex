ยง 25-2-592 - DRIVE-IN SERVICES.
(A) A business that offers a drive-in service must provide a similar service for a pedestrian that does not require the pedestrian to stand or walk in a vehicle
lane.
(B) For a drive-in service, the total width of the curb cuts on a block face may not exceed 30 feet.
Source: Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 030612-93; Ord. 031211-11.
