ยง 25-2-642 - CAPITOL VIEW CORRIDOR (CVC) OVERLAY DISTRICT REGULATIONS.
(A) In the Capitol view corridor (CVC) combining district, the maximum height permitted is the lesser of:
(1) the base district maximum height; or
(2) the maximum height provided in this section.
(B) In a Capitol view corridor, a structure may not exceed the elevation of the plane delineating the corridor. The height limitation exceptions of Section 25-2-
531 (Height Limitation Exceptions) do not apply to this subsection.
Source: Section 13-2-710; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20060216-043; Ord. 20060309-058; Ord. 20060622-022; Ord. 20060928-022.
