ยง 25-2-650 - CRIMINAL JUSTICE CENTER (CJC) OVERLAY DISTRICT REGULATIONS.
In the criminal justice center overlay district:
(1) a bail bond services use, cocktail lounge use, or liquor sales use that would otherwise be a permitted use is a conditional use; and
(2) a pawn shop services use is prohibited.
Source: Ord. 010426-48; Ord. 031211-11.
