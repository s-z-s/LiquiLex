(A) This subsection applies to the University/Deep Eddy subdistrict.
(1) The following uses are prohibited:
(a) automotive rentals;

(d) automotive washing;

be used for pedestrian-oriented uses. The Land Use Commission may allow an applicant up to five years from the date a certificate of occupancy is issued
to comply with this requirement.
