ยง 25-2-734 - EAST RIVERSIDE SUBDISTRICT REGULATIONS.
(A) This section applies in the East Riverside subdistrict of the WO combining district.
(B) The primary setback line is located 100 feet landward from the Town Lake shoreline.
(C) For an area not included in a primary setback area or a secondary setback area, the maximum impervious cover is 50 percent.
(D) The maximum height is the lower of 96 feet or the maximum height allowed in the base zoning district.
Source: Section 13-2-702(i); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20090611-074.
