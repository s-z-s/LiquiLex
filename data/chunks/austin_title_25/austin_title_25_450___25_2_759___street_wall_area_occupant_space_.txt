ยง 25-2-759 - STREET WALL AREA OCCUPANT SPACE.
(A) At least 42 percent of the street wall area of a building must contain occupant space.
(B) If a building has street wall areas on more than one street, at least 70 percent of the required occupant space must be on a street that runs east and
west.
(C) This section does not apply to a commercial off-street parking structure.
Source: Ord. 040902-58; Ord. 20080925-039.
