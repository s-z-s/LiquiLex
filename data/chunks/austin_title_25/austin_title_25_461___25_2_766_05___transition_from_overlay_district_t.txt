ยง 25-2-766.05 - TRANSITION FROM OVERLAY DISTRICT TO BASE DISTRICT.
(A) Until council approves a station area plan in accordance with Subpart C (Station Area Plan):
(1) a transit oriented development (TOD) district functions as an overlay district; and
(2) property within the TOD district:
(a) is subject to Subpart B (Initial District Regulations); and
(b) retains its base district zoning.
(B) The approval by council of a station area plan in accordance with Subpart C (Station Area Plan) is a rezoning of the property as a TOD base district. After
the rezoning, Subpart B (Initial District Regulations) does not apply.
Source: Ord. 20050519-008.
