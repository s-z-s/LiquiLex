(A) For a building with a front yard setback of 15 feet or less, parking is prohibited in the area between the front lot line and the building. The director of the

(1) the modification is required to protect a historic structure or a tree designated as significant by the city arborist; or

(B) For a rear parking lot on a site larger than three acres, the parking lot must be designed to permit future driveway and sidewalk connections with
adjacent non-residential property. The director may waive this requirement if the director determines:
(1) the connections are impractical because of site constraints;
(2) the connections are inappropriate because of traffic safety issues; or
(3) the site's land use is incompatible with the land use of the adjacent property.
