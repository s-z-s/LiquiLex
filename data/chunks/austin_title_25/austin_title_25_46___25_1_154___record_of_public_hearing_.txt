ยง 25-1-154 - RECORD OF PUBLIC HEARING.
(A) The body conducting a public hearing shall record each public hearing on audio tape or video tape.
(B) The official record of a public hearing includes:
(1) the audio tape or video tape recording of the public hearing;
(2) written staff reports; and
(3) documentary evidence submitted during a public hearing.
(C) A person may review the official record of a public hearing.
(D) The custodian of the records of the body conducting the hearing may establish rules regarding the time and location for review of the record.
