ยง 25-2-802 - ART GALLERY AND ART WORKSHOP USES.
(A) For an art gallery use in a general office (GO) or more restrictive district, the sale of art supplies, equipment, or accessories is prohibited.
(B) This subsection applies to an art workshop use in a community commercial (GR) or more restrictive district.
(1) The use may not:
(a) exceed 5,000 square feet of gross floor area; or
(b) produce external noise, vibration, smoke, dust, odor, heat, glare, fumes, electrical interference, or waste runoff.
(2) The following are prohibited:
(a) the outdoor storage of materials; and
(b) the use of welding equipment, fiberglass, or epoxy.
Source: Ord. 040617-Z-1.
