ยง 25-1-3 - CONFLICTS.
(A) Requirements of this title are cumulative of requirements that are imposed by other ordinances, rules, or regulations, or by private easements,
covenants, restrictions, or agreements. If a conflict occurs, the requirements of this title control.
(B) If there is a difference of meaning or implication between the text of a provision of this title and an illustration or table, the text controls.
Source: Sections 13-1-20 and 13-1-21(d); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
