ยง 25-2-810 - PRINTING AND PUBLISHING USE.
(A) This section applies to a printing and publishing use in a community commercial (GR) or more restrictive district.
(B) The use may not:
(1) exceed 5,000 square feet of gross floor area; or
(2) produce external noise, vibration, smoke, dust, odor, heat, glare, fumes, electrical interference, or waste runoff.
(C) The following are prohibited:
(1) the outdoor storage of materials; and
(2) the wholesale distribution of goods.
(D) The use is limited to printing equipment typically used in a business office.
Source: Ord. 040617-Z-1.
