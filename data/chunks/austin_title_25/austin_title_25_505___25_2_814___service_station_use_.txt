ยง 25-2-814 - SERVICE STATION USE.
A service station use:
(1) must be screened from the street by a building or a landscape buffer that includes shade trees;
(2) may not have more than 16 fuel dispensers; and
(3) may not have more than eight vehicle queue lanes.
Source: Ord. 20060831-068; Ord. 20110804-008.
