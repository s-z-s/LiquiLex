ยง 25-2-835 - SCHOOL DISTRICT DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENTS.
Development of an independent school district educational facility site may be governed by an agreement authorized by Section 212.902 of the Local
Government Code. If the City and an independent school district have executed an agreement, the terms of that agreement supersede the requirements of this title
and the criteria manuals to the extent of conflict.
Source: Section 13-2-620; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
