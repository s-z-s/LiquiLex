ยง 25-1-185 - NOTICE TO PRESIDING OFFICER AND APPLICANT.
On receipt of a notice of appeal or an amendment of a notice, the responsible director or building official shall promptly notify the presiding officer of the body to
which the appeal is made and, if the applicant is not the appellant, the applicant.
Source: Section 3-1-253(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
