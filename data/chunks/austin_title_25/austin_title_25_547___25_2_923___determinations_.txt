ยง 25-2-923 - DETERMINATIONS.
The building official may permit a temporary use after determining that the temporary use:
(1) will not impair the normal, safe, and effective operation of a permanent use on the same site;
(2) will be compatible with nearby uses;
(3) will not adversely affect public health, safety, or convenience;
(4) will not create a traffic hazard or congestion; and
(5) will not interrupt or interfere with the normal conduct of uses and activities in the vicinity.
Source: Section 13-2-323; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
