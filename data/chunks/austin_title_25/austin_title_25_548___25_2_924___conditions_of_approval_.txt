ยง 25-2-924 - CONDITIONS OF APPROVAL.
The building official may condition the approval of a temporary use on compliance with additional requirements that the building official determines are
necessary to ensure land use compatibility and minimize adverse effects on nearby uses, including requirements for hours of operation, frequency of use, parking
design, traffic circulation, screening, enclosure, site restoration, and cleanup.
Source: Section 13-2-322(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20231102-028, Pt. 20, 11-13-23.
