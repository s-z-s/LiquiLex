ยง 25-2-945 - ABANDONMENT OF NONCONFORMING USE.
(A) A person abandons a nonconforming use if:
(1) the person changes the use of property from a nonconforming use to a conforming use; or
(2) the person discontinues the nonconforming use for 90 consecutive days.
