ยง 25-2-948 - CERTAIN USES NEAR HAZARDOUS PIPELINES.
(A) This section applies to a use that is nonconforming because of its proximity to a hazardous pipeline under Section 25-2-516(B) (Development Near a
Hazardous Pipeline).
(B) Proximity to a hazardous pipeline does not cause a structure or area to be noncomplying under Article 8 (Noncomplying Structures).
(C) Except as provided by Subsection (C), the use is governed by Group "D" regulations prescribed by Section 25-2-947 (Nonconforming Use Regulation
Groups).
(D) If there is a conflict between the regulations prescribed by this section and the regulations as determined by Section 25-2-946 (Determination of
Nonconforming Use Regulation Group), the more restrictive regulations apply.
Source: Ord. 030410-12; Ord. 031211-11.
