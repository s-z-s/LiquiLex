ยง 25-2-961 - NONCOMPLYING DEFINED.
NONCOMPLYING means a building, structure, or area, including off-street parking or loading areas, that does not comply with currently applicable site
development regulations for the district in which it is located, but did comply with applicable regulations at the time it was constructed.
Source: Section 13-2-331; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
