ยง 25-2-962 - STRUCTURES COMPLYING ON MARCH 1, 1984.
(A) A structure that complied with the site development regulations in effect on March 1, 1984, is a complying structure notwithstanding the requirements of
this chapter.
(B) A structure that complies with the site development regulations does not become a noncomplying structure as the result of a change in the use, zoning,
or development of adjacent property.
Source: Section 13-2-820; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
