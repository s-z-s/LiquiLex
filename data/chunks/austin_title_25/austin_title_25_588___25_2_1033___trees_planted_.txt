ยง 25-2-1033 - TREES PLANTED.
(A) A tree planted under this division shall:
(1) be suitable, as defined in the Environmental Criteria Manual;
(2) meet the spacing and location requirements in the Environmental Criteria Manual; and
(3) be maintained in accordance with the Environmental Criteria Manual.
(B) The size of a tree planted under this division shall be:
(1) at least two inches in diameter measured six inches above root flare; or
(2) if an understory tree, at least one inch in diameter measured six inches above root flare.
Source: Ord. 20090618-077.
