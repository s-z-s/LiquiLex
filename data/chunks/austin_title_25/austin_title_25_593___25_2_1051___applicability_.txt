ยง 25-2-1051 - APPLICABILITY.
Except as provided in Section 25-2-1052 (Exceptions) or another specific provision of this title, this article applies to a site that is:
(1) zoned multi-family residence moderate-high density (MF-4) district or less restrictive district; and
(2) located within 75 feet of a triggering property.
Source: Ord. No. 20240516-004, Pt. 1, 7-15-24.
