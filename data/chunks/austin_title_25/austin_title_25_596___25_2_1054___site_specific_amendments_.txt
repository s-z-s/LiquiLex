ยง 25-2-1054 - SITE-SPECIFIC AMENDMENTS.
(A) Except as provided in Subsection (B), council may grant site-specific amendments to height and compatibility buffers if council determines that an
amendment is appropriate and will not harm the surrounding area.
(B) A site is not eligible for a site-specific amendment if the site is:
(1) zoned:
(a) a special purpose base zoning district;
(b) density bonus (DB) combining district; or
(2) subject to the university neighborhood overlay (UNO).
(C) A site-specific amendment to this article is considered a rezoning of property and is subject to the same requirements and procedures established for a
rezoning application that changes the base district classification of a property.
(D) A site-specific amendment may be initiated by:
(1) council;
(2) the Land Use Commission; or
(3) the record owner.
Source: Ord. No. 20240516-004, Pt. 1, 7-15-24.
