ยง 25-1-192 - POWER TO ACT ON APPEAL.
A body hearing an appeal may, in accordance with the requirements of this title, exercise the power of the official or body whose decision is appealed. A decision
may be upheld, modified, or reversed.
Source: Section 13-1-256; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
