In this article:
SCENIC VISTA means a generally recognizable, noteworthy view of:
(1) Barton Creek;
(2) Bull Creek;
(3) West Bull Creek;
(4) Lake Austin;
(5) Lake Travis;
(6) a valley of the Colorado River; or
(7) the downtown area of Austin.
Source: Section 13-2-783(b)(1); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
