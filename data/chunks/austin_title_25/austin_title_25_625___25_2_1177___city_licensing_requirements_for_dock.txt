ยง 25-2-1177 - CITY LICENSING REQUIREMENTS FOR DOCKS, MARINAS AND OTHER LAKEFRONT USES.
(A) A license agreement from the City is not required for a dock located along Lake Austin, Lady Bird Lake, or Lake Walter E. Long, regardless of any
easements or other ownership rights held by the City.
(B) No living quarters or business, including a marina, may be constructed into or above a lake that is subject to this article, unless the city council approves a
license agreement for the use after receiving a recommendation from the Land Use Commission.
Source: Section 13-2-796; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20101209-075; Ord. No. 20140626-113, Pt. 12, 7-7-14.
