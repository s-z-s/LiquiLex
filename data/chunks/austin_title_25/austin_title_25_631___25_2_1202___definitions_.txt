ยง 25-2-1202 - DEFINITIONS.
In this division:
(1) MOBILE HOME SPACE means an area in a mobile home park that is designed for and designated as the location for a single mobile home and the
exclusive use of its occupants.
(2) MOBILE HOME STAND means that portion of a mobile home space on which the mobile home is placed.
Source: Section 13-2-930; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
