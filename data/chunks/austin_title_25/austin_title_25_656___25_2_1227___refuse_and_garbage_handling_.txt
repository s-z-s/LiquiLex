The owner or licensee of a mobile home park shall provide for the storage, collection, and disposal of refuse in the park in a manner that does not create a health
hazard, rodent harborage, an insect breeding area, an accident or fire hazard, or air pollution.
Source: Section 13-2-962; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
