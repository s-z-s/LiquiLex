ยง 25-1-216 - EFFECTIVE DATE OF VARIANCE OR SPECIAL EXCEPTION.
(A) Except as provided in Subsection (B), a decision on a variance or special exception is effective immediately.
(B) If a variance or special exception is appealable, a decision on the variance is effective:
(1) except as provided in Subsection (B)(2), at the expiration of the time period during which an appeal may be filed; or
(2) if a notice of appeal is filed, when a final decision on the appeal is made.
Source: Section 13-1-285; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20110526-098.
