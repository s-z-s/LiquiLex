§ 25-1-218 - RESTRICTION ON SIMILAR APPLICATIONS.
If an application for a variance or special exception is denied or if a variance or special exception is revoked, a person may not file an application for the same or
a similar variance or special exception on the same or substantially the same site for a period of one year from the date of denial or revocation.
Source: Section 13-1-287; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20110526-098.
