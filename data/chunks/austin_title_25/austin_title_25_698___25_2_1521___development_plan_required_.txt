(A) A person may not use or develop property as a residential infill or neighborhood urban center special use unless the Planning Commission approves a
development plan under this division.
(B) A development plan must include:
(1) the locations of land uses, number of dwelling units, and approximate gross floor area of each use;
(2) the layout of the transportation network;
(3) the location, size, and type of each community open space area;
(4) the location and type of each drainage or water quality control facility;
(5) the location of the 100-year flood plain;
(6) the location of each critical environmental feature; and
(7) additional information required by the director of the Neighborhood Planning and Zoning Department to demonstrate compliance with this
subchapter.
Source: Ord. 000406-81; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
