ยง 25-2-1535 - DUPLEX REGULATIONS.
For a duplex residential use:
(1) the minimum lot area is 5,750 square feet;
(2) the minimum lot width is 50 feet;
(3) the minimum front setback is 15 feet;
(4) the minimum street side setback is 10 feet;
(5) the minimum interior side yard setback is five feet;
(6) the minimum rear yard setback is five feet;
(7) the maximum height is 35 feet;
(8) the maximum building coverage is 50 percent; and
(9) the maximum impervious coverage is 55 percent.
Source: Ord. 000406-81; Ord. 031211-11.
