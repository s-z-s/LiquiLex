ยง 25-2-1537 - MULTIFAMILY AND CONDOMINIUM REGULATIONS.
(A) For a multifamily residential use, including a condominium residential use:
(1) the minimum lot area is 5,750 square feet;
(2) the minimum lot width is 50 feet;
(3) the maximum front setback is 10 feet;
(4) the minimum front setback is five feet;
(5) the minimum street side yard setback is 10 feet;
(6) the minimum interior side yard setback is five feet;
(7) the minimum rear yard setback is 10 feet;
(8) the maximum building height is 35 feet;
(9) the maximum building coverage is 55 percent;
(10) the maximum impervious coverage is 65 percent;
(11) the maximum building footprint is 4,000 square feet; and
(12) a building may contain not more than 12 dwelling units.
(B) One hundred square feet of private open space is required for each multifamily dwelling.
(C) Two hundred square feet of private open space is required for each condominium dwelling.
(D) Parking is not permitted in a front yard.
Source: Ord. 000406-81; Ord. 031211-11.
