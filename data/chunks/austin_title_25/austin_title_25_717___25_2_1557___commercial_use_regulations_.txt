ยง 25-2-1557 - COMMERCIAL USE REGULATIONS.
For a commercial use:
(1) the minimum lot size is 3,500 square feet;
(2) the minimum lot width is 50 feet;
(3) the maximum height is 60 feet;
(4) the maximum front yard setback is 10 feet;
(5) the minimum front yard setback is five feet; and
(6) the minimum street side yard setback is 10 feet.
Source: Ord. 000406-81; Ord. 030424-57; Ord. 031211-11.
