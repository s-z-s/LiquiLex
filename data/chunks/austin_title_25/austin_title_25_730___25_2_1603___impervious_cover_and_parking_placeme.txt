ยง 25-2-1603 - IMPERVIOUS COVER AND PARKING PLACEMENT RESTRICTIONS.
(A) This section applies to a single-family residential use, a duplex residential use, or a two-family residential use.
(B) Except as provided in Subsection (C), impervious cover in a front yard may not exceed 40 percent.
(C) The director may waive Subsection (B) if the director determines backing a motor vehicle onto the adjacent roadway is unsafe and that a circular driveway
or turnaround in the front yard is required.
(D) Not more than four parking spaces may be located in the front street yard, or for a corner lot, not more than four parking spaces may be located in the
front street yard and side street yard combined.
Source: Ord. 030925-64; Ord. 031211-11.
