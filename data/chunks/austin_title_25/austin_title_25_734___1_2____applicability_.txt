1.2.1. General Applicability.
The applicability of this Subchapter varies by section and is dependent on the type of principal street that the subject lot or site faces and on the type of
development activity proposed. Table A summarizes the applicability of each section of this Subchapter. Only those sites and projects that meet both the principal
street and development type thresholds in the table are subject to the particular standard. General exemptions from the requirements of this Subchapter are listed
in Subsection 1.2.4., and additional exemptions from specific standards are listed in subsequent sections of this Subchapter.
