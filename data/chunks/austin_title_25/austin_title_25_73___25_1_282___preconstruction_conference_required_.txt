ยง 25-1-282 - PRECONSTRUCTION CONFERENCE REQUIRED.
(A) Except as provided in Subsection (C), the owner of a project, or owner representative, shall participate in a preconstruction conference with accountable
officials before starting construction under an approved site plan or subdivision construction plan.
(B) An owner, or owner representative, shall request the accountable official to schedule the preconstruction conference when the owner pays the required
inspection fees.
(C) The director may waive the requirement for a preconstruction conference.
Source: Section 13-1-831(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
