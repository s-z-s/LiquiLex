ยง 25-1-285 - MINUTES OF CONFERENCE.
Before construction begins, the owner's consulting engineer shall prepare and distribute minutes of the preconstruction conference. Conference participants
may file exceptions to the minutes. The engineer shall distribute copies of exceptions to the conference participants and shall include the exceptions in the
inspection file.
Source: Section 13-1-831(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
