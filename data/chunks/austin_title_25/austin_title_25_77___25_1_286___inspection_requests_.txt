ยง 25-1-286 - INSPECTION REQUESTS.
(A) The central dispatcher for the City shall coordinate contact between a permittee and an inspector.
(B) A permittee shall contact the central dispatcher to request an inspection.
(C) The accountable official may:
(1) require that a request be made 48 hours before the date the inspection is desired; and
(2) specify the manner in which the request is made.
(D) The central dispatcher shall maintain inspection requests for the city departments.
Source: Section 13-1-833; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
