ยง 25-3-22 - PRESUBMITTAL MEETING.
An application for zoning or rezoning to a traditional neighborhood district may not be accepted for filing before the applicant meets with the director or the
director's designee in a presubmittal meeting. The purpose of the meeting is to acquaint the city staff with the proposed development, provide the applicant with
preliminary staff comments, and identify major concerns or the need for additional data. Discussion topics at the meeting must include transportation, the
environment, drainage, land use, and design concepts.
Source: Section 13-9-12; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
