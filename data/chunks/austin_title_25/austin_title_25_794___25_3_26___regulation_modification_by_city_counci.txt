ยง 25-3-26 - REGULATION MODIFICATION BY CITY COUNCIL.
The city council may, at the time a traditional neighborhood district is created, modify the permitted uses or the site development regulations set forth in this
chapter. The modifications must be included in the ordinance zoning or rezoning the property. Modifications may be permitted only if justified by exceptional
circumstances and must be consistent with the character of the traditional neighborhood district.
Source: Section 13-9-16; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
