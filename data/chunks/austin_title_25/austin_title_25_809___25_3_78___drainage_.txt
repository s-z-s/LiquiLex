ยง 25-3-78 - DRAINAGE.
(A) The drainage provisions of the Land Development Code apply to development in a traditional neighborhood district, except as provided in this section.
(B) Drainage planning and engineering for a traditional neighborhood district shall be for the district as a whole.
(C) In designing drainage facilities, impervious cover calculations shall assume maximum impervious cover for each lot within the traditional neighborhood
district.
(D) Drainage facilities shall be privately owned, with easements granted to the City, and maintained for functionality by the City, unless other arrangements
are made with the director of the Watershed Protection and Development Review Department.
