ยง 25-3-81 - ROADWAY DESIGN.
(A) The roadway designs used within the different areas of the traditional neighborhood district may vary depending on the proposed function of the
roadway, the anticipated adjacent land uses, and the anticipated traffic load. The City encourages the use of a variety of designs to lend character to the
neighborhood.
(B) Roadway designs that may be used in a traditional neighborhood district are in the Traditional Neighborhood District Criteria Manual.
(C) The director may approve the use of innovative roadway designs that are not listed in the Traditional Neighborhood District Criteria Manual.
