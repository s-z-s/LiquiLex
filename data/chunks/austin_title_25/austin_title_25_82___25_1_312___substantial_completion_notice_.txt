ยง 25-1-312 - SUBSTANTIAL COMPLETION NOTICE.
(A) Approximately 10 days before work under the subdivision construction plans is finished, the owner shall notify the accountable official in writing that the
work is substantially complete and shall request a list of work to be completed.
(B) On the day that the owner provides notice under Subsection (A), the consulting engineer shall submit a construction summary report to the accountable
official.
Source: Section 13-1-861; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
