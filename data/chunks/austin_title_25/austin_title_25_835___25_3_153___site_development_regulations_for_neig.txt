ยง 25-3-153 - SITE DEVELOPMENT REGULATIONS FOR NEIGHBORHOOD EDGE AREA.
The site development regulations for a Neighborhood Edge Area are as follows:
(1) Minimum lot size: 5,750 square feet
(2) Minimum lot width: 50 feet
(3) Maximum height: 35 feet
(4) Minimum front setback: 25 feet
(5) Minimum street side yard setback: 15 feet
(6) Minimum interior side yard setback: 5 feet
(7) Minimum rear yard setback: 10 feet
(8) Maximum building coverage: 55%
(9) Maximum impervious cover: 65%
Source: Section 13-9-83; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
