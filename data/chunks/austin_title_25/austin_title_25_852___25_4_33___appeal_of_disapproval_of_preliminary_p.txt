§ 25-4-33 - APPEAL OF DISAPPROVAL OF PRELIMINARY PLAN AND PLAT.
If the director disapproves the preliminary plan or plat, the applicant can appeal the director's decision to the Land Use Commission or city council.
Source: Ord. No. 20230831-141, Pt. 19, 9-11-23.
Editor's note— Ord. No. 20230831-141, Pt. 20, effective September 11, 2024, repealed the former § 25-4-33, and enacted a new § 25-4-33 as set out herein. The
former § 25-4-33 pertained to administrative approval of certain subdivision applications and derived from Ord. No. 20190822-117, Pt. 21, 9-1-19.
