ยง 25-4-36 - VARIANCE FILING AND CONSIDERATION.
(A) If an application for a preliminary plan, plat requires a variance from a subdivision requirement, an applicant is required to obtain approval for the
variance before associated application for a preliminary plan, plat or subdivision construction plan can be approved by the director.
(B) The requirement of Section 25-1-214 (Public Hearing and Notice) that the Land Use Commission hold a public hearing not later than 45 days after the
date an application for a variance is filed does not apply to an application for a variance from a subdivision requirement.
