(A) Approval of a plat is not an acceptance by the City of an offered dedication. Disapproval or denial of a plat is a refusal by the City to accept an offered
dedication shown on a plat.
(B) The City may accept an offered dedication only by the action of an authorized official.

(D) A street may not be accepted unless it is surfaced, curbed, and guttered with the required utilities and drainage facilities installed. The City's entry, use, or
