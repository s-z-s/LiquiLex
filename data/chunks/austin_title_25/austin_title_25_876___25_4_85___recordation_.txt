ยง 25-4-85 - RECORDATION.
(A) The director shall record an approved plat in each county where land included in the plat is located.
(B) The director may not record a plat located in the extraterritorial jurisdiction unless the appropriate county has approved the plat.
(C) An applicant must provide all of the items and fees required to record the plat with the County Clerk within 80 business days from the date of plat
approval.
(D) An application for plat approval expires on the 81 st business day after such approval unless Subsection (C) of this section is satisfied.
Source: Section 13-1-493; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20160421-039, Pt. 8, 5-2-16.
