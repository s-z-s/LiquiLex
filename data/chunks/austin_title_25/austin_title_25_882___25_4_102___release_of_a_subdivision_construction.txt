ยง 25-4-102 - RELEASE OF A SUBDIVISION CONSTRUCTION PLAN.
(A) The director may release a subdivision construction plan if:
(1) the director approves the subdivision construction plan; and
(2) the applicant posts the required fiscal security with the director.
(B) The director's release of a subdivision construction plan authorizes the applicant to begin development in accordance with the plan.
Source: Section 13-1-494; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20230831-141, Pts. 31, 34, 9-11-23.
