(A) Easements for public utilities and drainage ways shall be retained in all subdivisions in the widths and locations determined necessary by the director. All
easements shall be dedicated to public use for the named purpose and shall be aligned to minimize construction and future maintenance costs.
(B) Off-street loading and unloading facilities shall be provided on all commercial and industrial lots, except in the area described in Subsection (C). The
subdivider shall note this requirement on a preliminary plan and a plat.
