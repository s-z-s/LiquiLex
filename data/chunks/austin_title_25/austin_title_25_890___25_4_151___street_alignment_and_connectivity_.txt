ยง 25-4-151 - STREET ALIGNMENT AND CONNECTIVITY.
Streets of a new subdivision shall be aligned with and connect to existing streets on adjoining property unless the director of the Transportation and Public
Works Department determines that the Comprehensive Plan, topography, requirements of traffic circulation, or other considerations make it desirable to depart
from the alignment or connection.
Source: Section 13-2-423; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20080214-095; Ord. No. 20230831-141, Pt. 36, 9-11-23.
