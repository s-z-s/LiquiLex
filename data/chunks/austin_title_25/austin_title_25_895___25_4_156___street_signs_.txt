ยง 25-4-156 - STREET SIGNS.
(A) The location of a street sign is determined by the most recent version of the Texas Manual On Uniform Control Devices.
(B) The subdivider shall provide the City with street name signs for street intersections with traffic signals.
(C) The subdivider shall provide and install pole mounted street name signs at street intersections without traffic signals.
Source: Section 13-2-488(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
