ยง 25-4-171 - ACCESS TO LOTS.
(A) Each lot in a subdivision shall abut a dedicated public street.
(B) Chapter 25-6, Article 6 (Access to Major Roadways And In Certain Watersheds) governs access to a lot:
(1) on a roadway designated as a major arterial, freeway, parkway, or expressway in the transportation plan or in a roadway plan approved by the
appropriate county; or
(2) on a Hill Country Roadway.
Source: Section 13-2-424; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 030306-48A; Ord. 031211-11.
