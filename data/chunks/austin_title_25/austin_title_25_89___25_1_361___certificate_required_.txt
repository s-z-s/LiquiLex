ยง 25-1-361 - CERTIFICATE REQUIRED.
(A) In the zoning jurisdiction and in a municipal utility district that has a consent agreement with the City requiring the issuance of a building permit, a person
may not use, occupy, or change the existing use or occupancy of a structure unless the building official has issued a certificate of occupancy for the
structure.
(B) In the planning jurisdiction:
(1) for development that does not require a site plan, a person may not use or occupy a structure unless the accountable official has issued a certificate
of compliance for the subdivision infrastructure; and
(2) for development that requires a site plan, a person may not use or occupy the development included in the site plan unless the accountable official
has issued certificates of compliance for the site plan and the subdivision infrastructure.
Source: Sections 13-1-903, 13-1-904, and 13-1-906; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
