ยง 25-4-193 - GAS LINES.
If natural gas from a public utility is available within 2,000 feet of a subdivision, the subdivider shall:
(1) prepare plans for installation of natural gas lines to serve each lot and install the portions of the lines that are under a street or alley; or
(2) place a note on the plat stating that natural gas lines have not been installed.
Source: Section 13-2-477; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
