ยง 25-4-198 - PRIVATE ON-SITE SEWAGE FACILITY.
A subdivision that is to be served by private on-site sewage facilities must comply with Chapter 15-5 (Private Sewage Facilities) of the City Code. The local health
authority shall review a preliminary plan or plat and report its findings to the Watershed Protection and Development Review Department.
Source: Section 13-2-482; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010329-18; Ord. 031211-11.
