ยง 25-5-42 - APPROVAL DATE.
If a site plan is approved, the approval date is:
(1) the last day that an appeal may be filed, if final approval is by the director or the Land Use Commission; or
(2) the date of council approval.
Source: Section 13-1-614(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11.
