ยง 25-5-142 - LAND USE COMMISSION APPROVAL.
Land Use Commission approval of site plan is required for:
(1) a conditional use;
(2) except as provided in Section 25-5-2 (Site Plan Exemptions), development in a Hill Country Roadway Corridor; and
(3) if otherwise required by this title.
Source: Section 13-1-660; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. 20130228-074.
