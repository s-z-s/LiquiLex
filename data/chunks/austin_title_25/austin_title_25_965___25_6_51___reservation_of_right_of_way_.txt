ยง 25-6-51 - RESERVATION OF RIGHT-OF-WAY.
(A) The City may, as a condition to approval of a site plan or subdivision, require the reservation of right-of-way that is reasonably likely to be acquired for
public use consistent with this article. To be subject to reservation, land must be located along a roadway designated in:
(1) the Transportation Plan;
(2) an approved collector plan; or
(3) an established capital improvement project located in the planning jurisdiction of the City.
(B) The extent and location of the right-of-way reserved under Subsection (A) must conform to the Transportation Plan, approved collector plan, or capital
improvement project.
Source: Section 13-5-8(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11; Ord. No. 20170302-077, Pt. 4, 3-13-17.
