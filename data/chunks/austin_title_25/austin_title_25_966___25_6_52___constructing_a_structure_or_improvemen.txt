ยง 25-6-52 - CONSTRUCTING A STRUCTURE OR IMPROVEMENT IN RIGHT-OF-WAY PROHIBITED.
Except as provided in Section 25-6-56 (Agreement For Temporary Use Of Reserved Right-Of-Way) and Section 25-6-81 (Waiver Request), a person may not erect a
structure or make an improvement in a reserved right-of-way.
Source: Section 13-5-8(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 000309-39; Ord. 031211-11.
