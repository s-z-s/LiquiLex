ยง 25-6-85 - AMENDING DEVELOPMENT APPLICATION.
(A) An applicant may amend a development application to exclude an improvement from a reserved right-of-way.
(B) If an applicant amends an application to exclude an improvement from a reserved right-of-way, the City may discontinue procedures to acquire the
reserved right-of-way.
Source: Section 13-5-17; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
