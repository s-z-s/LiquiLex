ยง 25-1-392 - INSPECTION.
(A) A permit holder must, as a condition of the permit, to allow City inspectors to enter and inspect the land or premises that is the subject of the permit.
(B) An applicant for an approval under this title shall agree in writing to allow City inspectors to enter and inspect the land or premises that is the subject of
the application during approval and development.
(C) Entry and inspection under this section must be at a reasonable time for the purpose of investigating or enforcing the requirements of this title.
(D) If the premises are occupied, the City inspector shall present the inspector's credentials and request entry. If the premises are unoccupied, the inspector
shall attempt to contact a responsible person and request entry.
Source: Section 13-1-74; Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
