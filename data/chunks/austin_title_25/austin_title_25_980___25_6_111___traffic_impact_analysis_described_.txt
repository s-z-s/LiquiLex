ยง 25-6-111 - TRAFFIC IMPACT ANALYSIS DESCRIBED.
A traffic impact analysis is a study that:
(1) provides information on the projected traffic generated by a proposed development;
(2) assesses the effect of the proposed development on a roadway near the development;
(3) identifies a potential traffic operational problem or concern and recommends an action to handle the problem or concern; and
(4) assesses the potential vehicular trips generated by other undeveloped sites in the established study boundary.
Source: Sections 13-5-42(a) and 13-5-42(b); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
