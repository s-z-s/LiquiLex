ยง 25-6-113 - TRAFFIC IMPACT ANALYSIS REQUIRED.
(A) Except as otherwise provided in Section 25-6-117 (Waiver Authorized), a person submitting a site plan application or a zoning or rezoning application
must submit a traffic impact analysis to the department if the expected number of trips generated by a project exceeds 2,000 vehicle trips per day.
(B) If the director determines that the traffic impact analysis does not comply with the requirements of this article, the director may require the applicant to
supplement the traffic impact analysis to address a deficiency.
(C) An applicant required to supplement an analysis under Subsection (B) must submit the required supplemental material before the 27th day before the
date on which the application is scheduled for action.
Source: Sections 13-5-43, 13-5-44(b), and 13-5-46(a); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
