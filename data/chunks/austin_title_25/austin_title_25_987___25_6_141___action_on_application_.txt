ยง 25-6-141 - ACTION ON APPLICATION.
(A) The council or director may deny an application if:
(1) the results of a traffic impact analysis demonstrate that a proposed development may overburden the City's street system; or
(2) the projected traffic generated by the project, combined with existing traffic, exceeds the desirable operating level established in Section 25-6-116
(Desirable Operating Levels for Certain Streets) on a residential local or collector street in the traffic impact analysis study area or the neighborhood
traffic analysis study area.
(B) The council or director may approve an application if the applicant has satisfactorily mitigated adverse traffic effects as required by this Title.
