ยง 25-6-143 - APPEAL OF DIRECTOR ACTION.
(A) An applicant may appeal the director's denial of a site plan application under Section 25-6-141 (Action On Application) to the Land Use Commission. An
applicant may appeal the decision of the Land Use Commission to the council.
(B) The Land Use Commission or the council may approve a site plan application if the Land Use Commission or council determine that the:
(1) applicant has satisfactorily mitigated adverse traffic effects; or
(2) additional traffic from the project has an insignificant effect on a residential street.
Source: Section 13-5-47(b)(2); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 010607-8; Ord. 031211-11.
