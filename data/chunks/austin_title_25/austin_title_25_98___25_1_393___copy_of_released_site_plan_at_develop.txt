ยง 25-1-393 - COPY OF RELEASED SITE PLAN AT DEVELOPMENT SITE.
(A) A contractor shall keep a copy of the released site plan at the development site and allow a City inspector to examine it on request.
(B) A contractor's failure to produce the copy of the released site plan on request by a City inspector is prima facie evidence that a released site plan does not
exist.
Source: Section 13-1-72(c); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
