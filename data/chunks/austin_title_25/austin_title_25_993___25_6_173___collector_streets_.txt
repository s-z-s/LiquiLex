ยง 25-6-173 - COLLECTOR STREETS.
(A) The director of the Watershed Protection and Development Review Department shall make recommendations to the Planning Commission regarding the
designation of collector streets.
(B) The Planning Commission shall designate collector streets after receiving the recommendations required under Subsection (A).
