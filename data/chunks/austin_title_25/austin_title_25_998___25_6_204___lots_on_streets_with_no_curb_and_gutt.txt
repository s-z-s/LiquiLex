ยง 25-6-204 - LOTS ON STREETS WITH NO CURB AND GUTTER.
(A) A lot in a subdivision designed with streets without curb and gutter must be one-half acre or more in size and have 100 feet or more of street frontage.
(B) Land designated in a preliminary plan as dedicated for open space or public right-of-way may not be used to calculate a lot size for a lot described in
Subsection (A).
Source: Section 13-5-56(d); Ord. 990225-70; Ord. 031211-11.
